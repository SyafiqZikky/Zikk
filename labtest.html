A SYSTEM DEVELOPER

WHAT IS SYSTEM DEVELOPER ?

A system developer is a professional responsible for designing, creating, and maintaining software systems, applications, or platforms that meet specific business or user needs. They work on various stages of the software development life cycle, from initial concept and design to implementation, testing, and ongoing maintenance.

THE CHARACTERISTIC

  • Problem-solving Skills
  • Attention to Detail
  • Adaptability
  • Collaboration and Communication
  • Creativity
  • Time Management
  • Testing and Debugging Skills
